Bridging the Gap Between Cancer Health & Science

Empowering communities through education, advocacy, and support to prevent, detect, and overcome cancer effectively.

Discover Our Impact

Vision

To ensure equitable access to cancer health access, information, and care for all, particularly underserved communities, empowering them to prevent, detect, and overcome cancer effectively.

Mission

To bridge the gap between cancer health and information by delivering accessible education, advocacy, and support to the communities that need it the most. We are committed to:

  • Providing accurate and culturally relevant cancer education.
  • Advocating for early detection, prevention, and access to care.
  • Building a network of cancer health advocates.
  • Partnering with researchers, healthcare providers, and policymakers.

Our Impact

School Outreach

Cervical Cancer & HPV Uptake

Our outreach program at Anglican Grammar School reinforced knowledge about cervical cancer among secondary school students, promoting the vital uptake of the HPV vaccination.

Stakeholders Meeting

Prostate Cancer Awareness

Educating community stakeholders in Ogbomosho about prostate cancer risk factors, early symptoms, and available prevention. We focus on breaking down stigmas and barriers to medical help.

Awareness Walk

Breast Cancer Awareness

Our volunteers hit the streets to spread vital information, breaking down myths, and demonstrating self-breast examination techniques. Fostering better understanding and preventive practices.
